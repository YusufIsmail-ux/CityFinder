Rustenburg:

Surrounded by the Magaliesberg Mountain Range,

Hartebeespoort Dam is an all round holiday

destination offering various sporting activities,

water sports and adventure activities. Other top attractions

include an aquarium, private zoo, a snake park and a cableway.

Magaliesburg Village is a quaint and historic village in

picturesque surroundings with indigenous woodland, valleys and rivers.
